Hero List

Alucard

Role: Fighter

A highly skilled swordsman known for his powerful life-steal abilities and burst damage.

Layla

Role: Marksman

A ranged hero with massive damage potential, perfect for long-range attacks.

Gord

Role: Mage

A powerful mage who uses destructive magic to deal massive damage to enemies from a distance.

Tigreal

Role: Tank

Tigreal excels at crowd control with his powerful knock-up skills. He can initiate team fights and protect his teammates with his large shield.

Lancelot

Role: Assassin

Lancelot is known for his mobility and quick burst damage. His skills allow him to dash in and out of fights, dealing massive damage to the enemy's squishy heroes.

Rafaela

Role: Mage

Rafaela is a healer and a supporter who can turn the tide of battle with her healing abilities and movement speed buffs. She is essential for keeping allies alive in the middle of a fight.

Hero Roles

  • Fighter - Heroes with balanced abilities who are capable of dealing decent damage and taking some hits.

  • Marksman - Ranged attackers who excel at dealing damage from a distance.
  • Mage - Heroes who use magical abilities to deal damage to enemies from afar.
  • Tank - Heroes who absorb damage and protect the team with high durability.
  • Assassin - Fast, high-damage heroes who specialize in eliminating key targets quickly.
  • Support - Heroes who provide healing, buffs, and utility to their teammates.
