Mobile Legends is a popular mobile game that brings fast-paced 5v5 battles. Explore the game, its heroes, guides, events, and news!


Brief History of Mobile Legends

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB) was released globally in July 2016 by Moonton. The game quickly became one of the most popular mobile esports titles, especially in Southeast Asia. Its accessible gameplay, regular updates, and massive competitive scene made it a go-to game for mobile gamers worldwide. With regular updates, new heroes, and seasonal events, Mobile Legends continues to be a major player in the mobile gaming world.
